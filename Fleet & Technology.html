Fleet and Technology

Overview

Our Fleet and Capabilities

Our fleet is designed to handle a wide range of logistics needs, from local deliveries to long-haul transport. With various vehicles specialized for different types of cargo and distances, we can efficiently support both large-scale and more tailored delivery solutions. This versatility allows us to offer reliable options for diverse client requirements.

Commitment to Reliability and Efficiency

We prioritize reliability, efficiency, and timely deliveries. Our fleet is equipped with modern technology that ensures precise tracking and route planning. This commitment to advanced logistics tools allows us to maintain on-time deliveries, meeting client expectations with consistent service.

Prime Movers

Our prime movers are essential for heavy-duty and long-haul transport, capable of pulling large trailers with a maximum gross weight of up to 44,000 kg. Designed for stability and high-capacity hauling, they are ideal for large, heavy shipments that require secure transport over extended distances. Each prime mover is equipped with advanced tracking systems and robust safety features, ensuring secure handling and timely delivery of cargo. With GPS tracking and anti-theft devices, we maintain constant visibility of these high-powered vehicles, providing clients with peace of mind and ensuring that each delivery reaches its destination safely and on schedule.

Curtain-Sided Trailers

Our curtain-sided trailers are versatile, durable, and designed to streamline loading and unloading processes, making them ideal for palletized goods or oversized items. With retractable curtains on both sides, these trailers offer easy access for side-loading, which is particularly beneficial for routes requiring frequent stops or multiple drop-offs. Capable of handling loads up to 24,000 kg, curtain-sided trailers provide ample cargo space while protecting goods from external elements. This flexibility, combined with weather-resistant curtains, enables secure and efficient transport for various types of freight, ensuring that each load is protected throughout its journey.

Box Trucks

Box trucks in our fleet are ideal for local and regional deliveries, particularly for fragile or sensitive items requiring enhanced protection. With enclosed cargo space capable of holding up to 5,000 kg, these trucks shield goods from weather and road conditions, making them perfect for smaller shipments or urban routes where maneuverability is key. Box trucks are equipped with secure locks and GPS tracking to ensure the safe and reliable transit of each load, providing an efficient solution for deliveries that need both security and flexibility.

Flatbed Trucks

Our flatbed trucks are specifically designed for oversized and irregularly shaped cargo, such as construction materials, machinery, and large equipment. With an open and flat surface, flatbeds allow for easy loading from any side, handling loads up to 20,000 kg depending on cargo dimensions. This adaptability makes them ideal for bulky items that do not fit within standard trailers, offering secure strapping and binding features to safeguard even the most unusually shaped items. Enhanced with real-time tracking and robust safety measures, our flatbed trucks provide reliable, secure transportation for clients with unique shipping needs.

Technology in Logistics

GPS Tracking and Real-Time Visibility

GPS tracking allows real-time visibility for both your team and customers, enabling them to track their shipments in real time. This technology helps in proactive issue management and provides accurate estimated arrival times.

Route Optimization Software

Advanced route optimization software reduces delivery times and fuel consumption. It helps identify the fastest or most efficient routes, especially for complex or multi-stop deliveries, ensuring timely and cost-effective logistics.

Mobile and Driver Apps

Our drivers use mobile apps and digital platforms to communicate, log delivery statuses, and receive real-time updates, ensuring smooth communication and delivery accuracy.
